About Mr. Alex Chatbot

Mr. Alex Chatbot is a text-based Large Language Model designed and developed by **Mr. Alex Company**. Our mission is to provide an intelligent, localized, and contextually aware digital assistant experience, bridging the gap between cutting-edge AI technology and user needs.

Core Features:

  • **Multilingual Support:** Responds in the user's language (Amharic or English).
  • **Multimodal Capability:** Can process both text prompts and images.
  • **Contextual Memory:** Maintains chat history for cohesive conversations.
  • **Mr. Alex Persona:** Follows a specific set of instructions and company guidelines.

The Creator: Dawit Kebede

The chatbot was coded by **Dawit Kebede**, the founder of Mr. Alex Company.

His journey in technology is driven by a strong passion for innovation. Dawit is the founder of Mr. Alex, a private company focused on Artificial Intelligence (AI) creations and Cyber Security services. The main vision of Mr. Alex is to contribute to making Ethiopia a leader in technology and cyber security.
